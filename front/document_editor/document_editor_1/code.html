Q3 Product Strategy

fiber_manual_record Live Collaboration

Executive Summary: Architectural Refinement

Our primary objective for Q3 remains the absolute synthesis of editorial design with functional scalability. We are moving away from the paradigm of "Tools for Workers" toward "Platforms for Architects." This shift requires a surgical focus on our core interface surface logic.

Internal Velocity

Targeting a 40% reduction in interaction latency across the document canvas by late August.

Design Precision

Implementing the 'No-Line' rule globally to enhance visual cognitive load management.

Key Strategic Pillars

  • check_circle
    Tonal Layering: We will replace traditional shadows with subtle background tonal shifts to define document hierarchy.
  • check_circle
    Asymmetric Layouts: Utilizing whitespace as a primary navigation element rather than secondary margins.
  • check_circle
    Ghost Interfaces: Floating toolbars that respond to gaze and intent rather than permanent screen real estate.

"Visualizing the new workspace paradigm through environmental context."
